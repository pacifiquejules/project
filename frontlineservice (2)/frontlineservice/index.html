About FRONTLINE Protection services

Founded in 2019 by Mubi Cyane, our company was built to provide structured, unarmed protection that prioritizes safety without escalation.

Rwanda’s government-registered security company delivering internationally trained, disciplined, and trusted 24/7 protection.

Our philosophy is prevention-first: identify risk early, apply discipline under pressure, and keep clients secure with clear communication and situational awareness.

  • Risk detection before escalation
  • Calm and controlled response in high-pressure moments
  • Professional client interaction at all times
